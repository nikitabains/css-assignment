About the song

The song "Like My Father" by Jax (USA) is a tribute to the
idea of true love founded on friendship and partnerships. The
chorus explicity expresses the need for a man that can offer the
same kind of love and care that a father showed for the singer's
own mother. The verses and bridge detail some of the desires the
singer has for a future romance: to come home to flowers,to share
a mutual respect and kindness, to dance in the living room like
young sweethearts, to take road trips in the summer, to make fun of
each other, and to teach their daughter the same values of true
love. Ultimately, the singer hopes to find a man that loves her
in the same way that her ownfather loves her mother..>
